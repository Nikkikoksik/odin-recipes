A simple recipes for you and your loved ones to create an easy pasta dish.

Ingredients

    Pasta

  • Spaghetti (or preferred long pasta)

    • Tomato Sauce

  • Canned crushed tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Onion (optional)

    • Seasoning

  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Dried oregano or Italian seasoning
  • Chili flakes (optional)

    • Finish & Garnish

  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Grated Parmesan or Pecorino cheese

    • Optional Add-ins (if you want variations later)

  • Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Mushrooms
  • Olives
  • Cherry tomatoes

Steps

  1. Bring Water to a Boil

    2. Fill a large pot with water, add a generous amount of salt, and bring it to a rolling boil.

  2. Cook the Pasta

    3. Add the spaghetti to the boiling water and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Reserve a small cup of pasta water, then drain the pasta.

  3. Prepare the Sauce Base

    4. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add finely chopped garlic (and onion if using) and sauté until fragrant and soft, not browned.

  4. Add Tomatoes

    5. Stir in crushed tomatoes or passata. If using, add a small amount of tomato paste to deepen the flavor.

  5. Season the Sauce

    6. Add salt, black pepper, dried oregano (or Italian seasoning), and optional chili flakes. Let the sauce simmer gently for 10–15 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  6. Combine Pasta and Sauce

    7. Add the cooked pasta directly to the sauce. Toss well to coat, adding reserved pasta water if needed to loosen the sauce.

  7. Finish the Dish

    8. Remove from heat. Tear fresh basil leaves and mix them into the pasta.

  8. Serve

    9. Plate the pasta and top with grated Parmesan or Pecorino cheese. Serve immediately.
