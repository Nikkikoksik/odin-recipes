A simple recipe for you to make a hot cocoa.

Ingredients

    Hot Cocoa

  • Milk
  • Cocoa powder
  • Sugar
  • Dark or milk chocolate
  • Vanilla extract(optional)
  • Salt(a small pinch)

    • Toppings(Optional)

  • Whipped cream
  • Marshmallows
  • Chocolate shavings or drizzle
  • Cinnamon or nutmeg
  • Candy cane (for a seasonal version)
  1. Heat the Milk

    2. Pour the milk into a small saucepan and heat over medium-low heat until warm, but not boiling.

  2. Add Cocoa and Sugar

    3. Whisk in the cocoa powder and sugar until fully dissolved and smooth.

  3. Melt the Chocolate (Optional)

    4. If using chocolate, add it to the saucepan and stir until completely melted.

  4. Season the Cocoa

    5. Add a pinch of salt and a few drops of vanilla extract. Stir well.

  5. Simmer Gently

    6. Let the cocoa heat gently for 2â€“3 minutes, stirring occasionally. Do not let it boil.

  6. Serve

    7. Pour the hot cocoa into a mug.

  7. Add Toppings

    8. Top with whipped cream, marshmallows, chocolate drizzle, cinnamon, or a candy cane as desired.
